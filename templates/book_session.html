What are Mock Sessions

Mock sessions are structured practice consultations that simulate real-world GP scenarios and exam conditions. These sessions are designed to help you:

  • Build confidence in managing diverse patient cases effectively.
  • Hone your clinical reasoning and diagnostic skills.
  • Master the art of clear and empathetic communication with patients.
  • Identify and address knowledge gaps in a supportive learning environment.

With our Mock sessions, you can approach your GP career or exams with confidence and competence.
