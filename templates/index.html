About Us

We offer aspiring GPs the opportunity to develop their knowledge and Clinical skills including Consultation skills which ultimately will be needed in day to day consultations and SCA examinations.

This service is provided by experienced GPs and will allow trainees to find Exam practice Partners, study group and engaging with groups of GPs with wealth of Experience.

Telephone Consultations

Master telephone consultation skills for both routine practice and examinations.

Video Consultations

Learn effective video consultation techniques for modern medical practice.

Book Mock Sessions

Mock sessions are structured practice consultations that simulate real-world GP scenarios and exam conditions, designed to help you build confidence, enhance clinical reasoning and diagnostic skills, master clear communication, address knowledge gaps, and prepare effectively for exams; each session consists of two stations per hour, takes place every Thursday.
